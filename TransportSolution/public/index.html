Welcome to DePauw Transportation Support

This website provides a comprehensive solution for transportation needs. Here's a summary of the features:

  • Classifications of all available transportation: Rural transits/local drivers.
  • Contact information for services.
  • Matching with Drivers
    • Drivers submit forms detailing their availability, locations, capacity, and preferences for roundtrip or one-way trips.
    • Passengers share information about their travel time, group size, and trip preferences.
    • Drivers transport passengers to their destinations under convenient and agreed-upon conditions.
  • Bicycle Rental:
    • Track available bicycles and provide safety equipment (e.g., helmets).
    • Free rentals for specific time blocks; additional fees for extended usage.
    • Schedule rentals in advance.
