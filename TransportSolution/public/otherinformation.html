Other Information

This section provides additional details about transportation in Indiana, with a focus on Greencastle. It includes information on public transit, ride-sharing, shuttles, and other services to help you navigate both the state and the local community effectively.

Public and Regional Bus Services

IndyGo: The primary public bus system in Indianapolis, providing over 30 routes, including paratransit services. Tickets can be purchased online or at transit centers. Visit IndyGo for routes and schedules.

Greyhound and FlixBus: Provide long-distance bus services connecting Indianapolis to other cities in Indiana and across the U.S. Greyhound and FlixBus are available for bookings.

Airport Shuttles

GO Express Travel: Offers shuttles and private car services to Indianapolis International Airport (IND) from cities like Bloomington and Lafayette. Learn more.

Columbus Express: Previously operated shuttle services and now provides private airport transportation options. Explore options.

DePauw University: Offers shuttle services for students to nearby cities and airports. Contact the university for more details or check shuttle schedules via CampusLab or Corq.

Trains

Amtrak: Operates routes such as the Cardinal and Hoosier State Line, connecting Indianapolis with Chicago and other destinations. Tickets and schedules are available on the Amtrak website.

Ride-Sharing and Local Shuttles

Lyft/Uber: Widely available in cities and towns across Indiana, including Greencastle.

Local University and Community Shuttles: DePauw University in Greencastle may offer shuttle services for students.

Flights and Deals

Google Flights and Expedia: Provide comprehensive flight deals from Indianapolis and nearby airports. Compare ticket prices easily on platforms like Google Flights or Expedia.

Carpooling and Vanpooling

Commuter Connect: A resource for organizing carpools and vanpools, especially for daily commutes in Central Indiana. Learn more.
