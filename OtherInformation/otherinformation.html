Our Services

DePauw University Transportation offers a variety of services to make travel easier for students and staff:

  • Shuttle services to Indianapolis Airport.
  • Local transportation for campus and city destinations.
  • Special event and group transportation upon request.

Schedules

Our shuttles operate at the following times:

  • Monday-Friday: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM
  • Saturday: 10:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Sunday: 12:00 PM - 8:00 PM
